My name is Andrew Gavin and I am currently a Student persuing an MSc in Precision Cancer Medicine at the University of Oxford.
I am a highly motivated individual with a strong academic background and a passion for biomedical research.

Continuously expanding my knowledge and skillset, I have completed several online courses to improve my data analytical skills and some of my completed projects are availible in the Projects Section.

In terms of work experience, I have held various positions that have further shaped my skills and interests. Furthermore, I interned at St. Vincent's University Hospital, shadowing Dr. David Fennelly in the Oncology Department. I assisted the consultant in patient care, reviewed patient histories, and performed basic lab tests, gaining valuable insights into the clinical aspects of cancer treatment.
